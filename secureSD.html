Secure Software Development

This module introduces students to the underpinning concepts and skills of Secure Software Development.

Learning Outcomes

  • Identify and manage security risks
  • Identify development problems and solve them using appropriate tools/techniques
  • Develop the skills required to be an effective member of a development team
